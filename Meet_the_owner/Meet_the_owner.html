Meet the owner!

Hello my name is Ann Downie and I own the cafe Villa 59. I have been in the catering business for the past 3 years where before I had a cafe in South Queensferry. Before I went into catering I was a dental nurse for 15 years and worked all around West Lothian, but I knew I needed a career change so I moved into catering. When you come into my cafe I give a very warm welcome and my hopes for the future are to expand the cafe so as to increase staff numbers and to be open for longer. I'm very much looking forward to seeing you here!
